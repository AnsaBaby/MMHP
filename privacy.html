Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026. This privacy policy explains how the MyMaintenance pre-launch website handles personal information collected through waitlist and contact forms.

1. Who we are

This website is for MyMaintenance, a pre-launch property maintenance and digital property passport service.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact details such as name and email address.
  • Role or audience type, such as homeowner, landlord, or trade partner.
  • Postcode or service area for launch catchment and trade coverage checks.
  • Messages submitted through the contact form.
  • Technical information that may be collected by hosting, analytics, security, or form tools once connected.

3. How we use information

  • To manage early access and launch waitlists.
  • To assess postcode catchment demand and trade partner coverage.
  • To respond to enquiries and support requests.
  • To improve the website, service proposition, and launch communications.
  • To meet legal, security, and record-keeping obligations.

4. Lawful basis

For pre-launch enquiries and waitlist management, we expect to rely on consent, legitimate interests, or steps taken before entering a contract, depending on the final collection flow.

5. Sharing and processors

We may use trusted service providers for hosting, forms, email, analytics, security, and customer communication.

6. Retention

Waitlist and enquiry data should be kept while it is needed for launch validation, onboarding, support, legal, or operational purposes.

7. Your rights

Under UK data protection law, individuals may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict, object to processing, and request portability of their personal data. They may also complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact page.
